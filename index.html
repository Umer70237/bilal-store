About the Clinic

Founded decades ago by the late Dr. Abdul Qadeer, this clinic and medical store has been a trusted name in Balkassar for generations. Known for providing relief to daily health problems like fever, flu, cough, pain, and blood pressure issues, the clinic became a part of people's daily life.

Today, the clinic is run with the same care and dedication by his son Bilal, who continues to help the community with basic checkups and trusted advice at affordable prices. Located in the heart of Doctor Qadeer Street, the shop is open almost all day and remains a go-to place for local health needs.
